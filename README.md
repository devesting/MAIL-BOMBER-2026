
A **private, lightweight mass mailing tool** built for controlled distribution, testing, and internal campaigns.  
Not public. Not noisy. Just efficient.

---


**Private Mass Mailer** is designed for users who need to send emails at scale **responsibly and discreetly**.

Built with:
- Control over delivery
- Privacy-first mindset
- Minimal footprint
- No unnecessary bloat

This tool is **not intended for abuse** — it’s for people who know what they’re doing.

---


-  Bulk email sending  
-  Custom templates  
-  Queue & batch control  
-  Delay & rate limiting  
-  Import recipient lists  
-  Private / local use  

---


- Internal announcements  
- Opt-in newsletters  
- Testing email infrastructure  
- Development & QA  
- Controlled outreach  

If you’re looking to spam — this isn’t it.

---

1. Configure your mail server
2. Load your recipient list
3. Choose or edit a template
4. Set rate limits & delays
5. Send & monitor

Simple flow. Full control.
